the inspiration for the story

In a forest that's just a stone's throw away - (unless you've got a terrible throwing arm, in that case, pack your bags for a roadtrip) there resides a dazzling species of synchronous fireflies called “Photinus Carolinus.” Every summer, these lovestruck luminaries woo their mates with a romantic morse code of synchronized flashing patterns and put on a light show that would make even the legendary Studio 54 disco ball green with envy.

These glowing Romeo and Juliets are living the dream in the Smoky Mountain Forest of Tennessee, where trees are tall, the air is crisp as a fresh mint julep and the moonshine flows like a waterfall after a rain storm.

Now, everybody LOVES a good underdog story, right? So, we thought it would be fun to cook up a tale (pun totally intended) around this REAL LIFE phenomenon, a synchronous society of fireflies, and create a character who has been dealt a hand that makes synchronization with his blinking world all but impossible.

It's a feel-good story for all the square pegs in round holes, the odd ducks, the black sheep, the “why can't I just fit in folks” out there. A rallying cry to not only endure difficult circumstances but to take a victory lap around them. An invitation to embrace those quirks, missteps and faceplants, the things that make us ... well, us. Trusting in that cosmic DJ spinning tunes above, that somehow, against all odds, we too, can find our way back home, to ourselves and to our own unique light. And hey, in the meantime, we'll keep the porch light on for ya, just in case.

"one little firefly, one big problem, one grand adventure!"

our worlds

our human world:

Tucked away in the quaint town of ELKMONT, TENNESSEE, a place once buzzing with life, now clinging to it's faded charm. Here, in this tiny little nook, dreams have outgrown the map, yet hope springs eternal — porch lights flicker, hand-painted signs peek from windows and it's the kind of place where everyone knows your name. But beneath the hush lies a town itching for it's big comeback, ready to shine if someone will just grant it a stage.

our forest world:

Nestled in PIGEON FORGE, TENNESSEE, home to the fabled passenger pigeons and legendary Dollywood theme park, a secret luminary society thrives under the moon's gaze. Fireflies flash in perfect harmony — a rhythmic glow, transforming the night into a living light show. These little luminaries dance alongside a kaleidoscope of forest creatures. Our Woodland Cast features dragonfly FBI directors, charade-playing spiders, and beavers hosting their own cooking channels. But when one of their own forest creatures mysteriously vanishes, the woodland gang springs into action.

It's here, at the edge of Great Smoky Mountain National Forest Range, our two worlds collide in a moment of magical synchronicity.

Meet the Cast

hover to illuminate each character

Bean Sparks

This pint-sized little chappy burst onto the scene early, born three-weeks premature and as of yet, HIS TAILLIGHT REFUSES TO TURN ON! Despite his charm and tender nature, he struggles to fit into his society that demands conformity. But with a headlamp gifted by his beloved grandfather and the guidance of a mystical family legend, Bean might just discover the nightlight he needs in his lightless, confusing little bug life.

June Shiney

A real spitfire-fly who thumbs her nose at her society's cookie cutter rulebook, but beneath her fierce facade hides a fragile heart. Just as she's ready to ditch camp, fate throws her into Bean's orbit, a kindred soul who spots the glitter beneath her armor.

Arlo Huxley

Meet Arlo, a young lad who, in a mad dash for a science fair project, nabs the fireflies, discovering these glowing little wonders could help salvage his shaky 7th-grade reputation.

Wilhameena Nannyhoot

A cornbread cookin' country-fried Maria Von Trapp, a fame-hungry nanny with big Dollywood Dreams!

Stanley D. Bucktooth

A quick-witted Smoky Mountain dwelling beaver with a twang in his drawl and a twinkle in his eye. By day, he gnaws timber, by night he hosts a cooking show called “Foraging the Forest” for any forest critter who happens by his dam. When the firefly kiddos vanish, he finds himself in the hot seat with the bug feds — after all, he was the last to see the young campers alive.

FBI Director Doug Darner

The top bug and rockstar of the FBI's buzzing Insect division! Think James Bond with a thorax, chasing down six-legged suspects. His bumble profile proudly lists “Darn!” as his signature expletive. As he tackles the case of the missing fireflies, he falls for his deputy's sister, Mariposa Golding, much to Serafina's dismay.

Dolly Parton

The reigning Queen of Country, a philanthropic powerhouse, an icon and a TRUE Smoky Mountain Original. She's the muse behind this very project! Our fabulous four dazzle the crowd beneath a canopy of trees when — BOOM! — Dolly herself waltzes in, enchanting everyone with her angelic voice.

Agent Serafina Golding

A true blue FBI agent and sister to the flamboyant Mariposa, juggling her badge and bug duties while always striving to wow the big boss. When two firefly campers vanish into the night, Serafina might just have to swallow her pride and enlist her sister's star power to help crack the case.

Mariposa Golding

A glitzy billionaire beauty tycoon and social media obsessed butterfly fluttering through the internet, racking up likes and shares and whose viral fame might just hold the key to finding the missing fireflies!

synopsis

Bean Sparks has always been different — Born into a society of SYNCHRONIZED FIREFLIES but LACKING the ONE thing they all have — A WORKING TAILLIGHT!!!

After a bumpy ride through school, Bean's shipped off to “Camp Glow Up Already!” where he meets JUNE SHINY, A REBELLIOUS SPITFIREFLY who SCOFFS at their society's COOKIE CUTTER RULES. June helps Bean tap into his hidden potential, and he, in turn, sees her INNER SPARKLE.

But just when things are starting to click, the duo get LOST IN THE WOODS and blunder into the path of STANLEY D BUCKTOOTH, A CHARISMATIC BEAVER who runs a serious side-hustle, hosting a cooking show for any forest creature who wanders by his dam. The two misfits are captured by ARLO HUXLEY, a human boy, scrambling for a last-minute science fair project.

THEIR PREDICAMENT WORSENS when Arlo's fame-hungry nanny, WILHAMEENA NANNYHOOT, sees the fireflies as USEFUL PROPS at her shot at fame. Wilhameena LOCKS THE FIREFLIES UP IN A JAR, planning to SHOWCASE THEM at an audition for the Tiny Town Talent Show, a live televised event that could CATAPULT HER TO STARDOM.

BACK AT CAMP, CHAOS ERUPTS — MISSING CAMPERS? THIS HAS NEVER HAPPENED BEFORE IN THE HISTORY OF CAMP!!! A DARING RAG TAG TEAM of FOREST CRITTERS BAND TOGETHER TO LAUNCH A WILD RESCUE MISSION, RACING against time to FREE Bean and June from WILHAMEENA's CLUTCHES.

With the fireflies now gone, the town SCRAMBLES to cobble together their show WITHOUT their award-winning act but just as the tv show begins, DISASTER STRIKES — a total BLACKOUT of the electrical system PLUNGES the forest STAGE and LIVE TV SHOW into utter DARKNESS!

Stanley, realizing the humans are about to RUIN his forest's fine REPUTATION, RUSHES BACK to camp to convince BEAN AND JUNE along with the REST OF THE FIREFLY COMMUNITY to RETURN to RE-LIGHT the stage to save the SINKING SHOW. Bean, with his BRILLIANT HEADLIGHT, LEADS THE CHARGE through the RUGGED FOREST TERRAIN hoping they can make it back before it's too late.

In a stunning DISPLAY of BIOLUMINESCENT MAGIC, the fireflies RE-LIGHT the stage, and Bean and June make a TRIUMPHANT RETURN with a SURPRISE performance that captivates the audience. Just when the night couldn't get any bigger, DOLLY PARTON, HERSELF, graces the stage leaving the crowd in AWE. And in the end, in a world that once called him DIFFERENT, Bean finally finds a place where he truly belongs — JUST EXACTLY AS HE IS.

Act One

Born Into a Synchronous Society

BEAN SPARKS is a firefly born into a synchronous world where blinking to the same beat means survival — but he's missing the one thing they all have: A WORKING TAILLIGHT! His family loves him fiercely, but his strict lockstep society labels him defective.

After a bumpy ride through school, Bean's packed off to “Camp Glow Up Already!” the ultimate glow-getter bioluminescent bootcamp. Enter JUNE SHINY, a rebellious spitfire-fly who's not too keen on the cookie-cutter mold she's supposed to fit into. June doesn't see Bean as broken at all, but instead sees his hidden potential, and he, in turn, sees her true inner sparkle.

After a terrifying high-flying escapade, the two enroll in a course in night sound identification. But when a bone-chilling shriek pierces the night, both counselors and campers scatter like bowling pins, leaving Bean and June alone and lost in the woods. Left to their own devices, the bewildered backpackers blunder into the path of STANLEY D. BUCKTOOTH — a charismatic beaver who hosts a cooking show called “Foraging the Forest.”

Act Two

Captured by the Humans

While Stanley is out hunting chicory root to rouse a snoozing June, the fireflies are captured by ARLO HUXLEY, a human boy scrambling for a last-minute science fair project. When Arlo's best bud CAL mocks his project — “JUST SOME BORING BUGS” — the fireflies take ROYAL OFFENSE and vow to outshine Cal and his soap volcano.

They secretly learn a dance routine from WILHAMEENA NANNYHOOT, Arlo's fame-hungry nanny, and perform it at the science fair — stunning the class like tiny synchronized swimmers at a rock concert! They steal the show and win first prize. But Nannyhoot, furious at being upstaged, concocts a scheme to steal back her limelight by entering the TINY TOWN TALENT SHOW, a national TV talent show contest — with the fireflies locked up in a jar as her star act.

Act Three

A New Plan for Fame

Nannyhoot splices in the dancing fireflies along with her and Arlo's dance routine to create a formidable audition tape — and Elkmont wins a shot to audition before the talent scouts! Meanwhile, back at camp, chaos erupts. MISSING FIREFLIES? THIS HAS NEVER HAPPENED BEFORE IN THE HISTORY OF CAMP!!! The Insect FBI are called in and a battalion of bugs, birds and butterflies launch a covert rescue mission.

During Elkmont's scouting audition, the bug rescue team swoops in and plucks Bean and June from the stage right before Elkmont's big encore and spirits them back to the safety of basecamp. But now the town's left without their star winning act a mere 24-hours before showtime, while Nannyhoot and Arlo race to train replacement bugs — but unfortunately, these understudies are truly “just boring bugs.”

The Finale

Stanley Vows to Save the Sinking Show

Stanley strikes a deal: he'll bring back the REAL dancing fireflies to perform, if he gets a prime spot on the night's show bill. Just as the deal is sealed, the show begins and Cal's volcano explodes onto the electrical system, plunging the tv show and audience into complete darkness. Stanley steals a security golf cart to race back to camp to convince the ENTIRE community of fireflies to return to relight the stage.

Moved by Stanley's plea, Bean rallies his firefly community to re-light the forest stage. Like a grand parade, the fireflies flank the golf cart, creating a mystical moving wall of light with Bean's headlight leading the way. The fireflies RE-LIGHT the stage, and Bean and June make a TRIUMPHANT RETURN. Then — DOLLY PARTON, HERSELF, steps out onstage and joins the four singing her song. Dolly awards the four dancers a special invitation to join her at DOLLYWOOD. And in the end, in a world that once called him different, Bean finally finds a place where he truly belongs — just exactly as he is.
